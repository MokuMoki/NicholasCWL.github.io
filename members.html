Started from nothing, now he's here. He was a normie Minecraft player when he was 13, residing in his friend's invite-only server but managed to exploit many part of the world resources to make him rich. During the age of 14, he moves to a public Survival server, built a village relatively far away from the spawn point with his newly formed guild, still managed to overtake most players in the server in terms of resources. During the next few years, he take initiative in large projects such as removing an Extreme Hills, building 100x100 farm and building a 4000 blocks distance long railway all through survival mode.

He is a proud server founder (Was CityForUs, now known as SpaceForUs) and a plugin developer. Currently, he is developing his brand new space-themed Minecraft server: SpaceForUs alongside his equally skilled friends. With his multi-year experience in Java programming, he contributed a lot in the core server features development. He is also well known for engaging in PvP fights in Minecraft, acquired skills such as jitter clicking and strafing.

Hosted a GTA San Andreas Multiplayer Server when he was 13 using a low-performing laopt on a Dynamic DNS domain name that is provided by his father CCTV manufacturer. Although the domain name suggested blurry vision (Blurguard), his vision isn't blur for his future. He managed to visit the Google Malaysia office in 2015 by showing-off his non-script kiddie level programming skill in a scripting competition. At his age of 18, he managed to earn some quick bucks through bug bounty by looking for security vulnabilities in a website and report it to webmaster.
