Jungling Map Guide

    

Creep Stacking

This is a method to gather more creeps in a jungle spot by pulling/luring the creeps out from their initial spot. To pull/lure the creeps, you need to initiate an attack towards one of the creeps to make them aggresive. Creeps in the jungle spawn every even minute mark such as (2:00), (4:00) and so on.. Creep Stacking needs to be done by pulling/luring the creeps 7s before the new batch of creeps spawn or 9s if the camp is well stacked because creeps will spawn in their own places if the previous creeps weren't around their own camp.

Creep Pulling

This method also requires precise timing in order to achieve the result. Creep pulling is where you pull/lure the creeps from the jungle into the nearest lane few seconds before the creeps that are going to that lane arrives. Also make sure that you get the last hit on the jungle creeps before your creeps kill them. This is mostly done in top lane of Dire or bot lane of Radiant. You should also pull/lure creeps from the Medium Camp in enemy's lane to let creeps fight each other although it might be risky and easy to get invasion from enemy as this trick can easily pull off because of your position. So make sure you be alerted on the lane you are jungling.
