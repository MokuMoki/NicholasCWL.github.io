A Goal Beyond Your Imagination

APU Extreme Esports Club was founded on 10th of June 2016. Our club was built to bring innovation to the Esports industry and inspire more people around the world to join Esports. We also invite professional Esports coaches to coach our members to become top-tier players in the world. However, meeting the minimum qualification is required to join our club. There are always tournaments awaiting for our members to participate so that our members could gain experience from hours of gameplay with skilful and worthy opponents.

Achievements



Remarkable records of competitive success that we have achieved:
  • Champion place on Acer Predator League Malaysia (PUBG) 3rd → 4th of November 2018
  • Champion place on Cyber Games Tournament (Mobile Legends, Dota 2, CSGO) 26th → 28th of October 2018
  • 2nd place on Taiwan Excellence Battleground Intercollege Grand Finale (League of Legends) 26th → 28th of October 2018
  • 3rd place on ESL Pro League Season 7 (CSGO) 13th of February → 20th of May 2018
  • Champion on Dota 2 Asia Championships 29th of March → 7th of April 2018
  • 3rd place on ELEAGUE Major (CSGO) 12th → 28th of January 2018
