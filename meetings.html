Lessons

Our lessons focuses on tips and tricks to sharpen our members' gaming skills. We update members on the latest techniques available fresh from the web, so everyone can stay on par with the top in the industry. From time to time, we have test matches to evaluate every members' potential and find out their strength and weaknesses, and provide the appropriate assistance and training to make the perfect gamer, to be the best of the best.





Competitive Gaming

We are all about being competitive while having fun at the same time. With weekly mini events like DOTA Tournaments and Minecraft Hunger Games, every player should be able to improve themselves over time, striving to have the highest win rates. At the end of every lesson, we will analyse the best and the worst out of every player, and understand what makes a champion different. Each team will compete as a fairly balanced team.
