Rules and Regulations

Our club have generally more lenient rules than the OG Esports Club. Although some rules are meant to be broken, these aren’t. To ensure you not getting kick out of the club or VAC banned, please abide to the following rules.

  1. Please wear proper attire during our meeting. Follow the guidelines here.
  2. Show your enthusiasm.
  3. Practice team work.
  1. No feeding.
  2. No friendly fire.
  3. No cheating.
  4. You may use offensive language during game matches to express your frustration, however, do not openly insult another player’s:
    • gender
    • identity
    • physical ability
    • origin
    • religion
    • sexual orientation
  5. Have a stable connection before joining matches.
  1. Camp near the spawn site to own respawns.
  2. Must teabag on your opponents to show who's the boss. This can be achieved by repeatedly crouching over the opponents.
  3. Always jump at military base.
  4. Blame lag when you lose.
  5. Blame the opponents for hacking when team lose.
