Attire and Equipment

Attire

We do not have an official attire that resemble the club, however, we do have a dress code to follow when attending meeting, which is the APU official dress code. (Appendix 1.1)

Dress code: Smart
  • Clothes must be ironed, smart and neat.
  • No casual outfits like T-shirts, collarless shirt, jeans, slippers or sneakers.
  • No hair dyeing with unnatural colors.
  • Female students are to dress modestly and discreetly. No see-through materials or short skirts.
  • All students must wear their student nametags prominently.

Equipment

Completely optional. However to be the ultimate pwn master, you may bring your own:
  • Razor Gaming Mouse
  • Razor Gaming Mouse Pad
  • Razor Gaming Keyboard
  • RGB LED strip
  • Gaming chair
  • G-Fuel
  • Gaming Monitor with crosshair support
  • Your own Razor Blade Gaming laptop
  • or a RTX capable Desktop PC
