DOTA 2 Tournament

After much planning by the APU Extreme eSports Club, we are pleased to announced that the APU DOTA 2 Competition (2019) has finally gathered enough support to make it possible. Tempted? Let's go through the prizes first:

Place Prize
1st RM1000
2nd RM500
3rd RM300
4th RM200

The competition is open to 16 teams only, each with 5 members. Teams will be selected based on registration form submission timestamp and qualifying teams will contacted by our event staff. A fee of RM50 is payable upon confirmation of entry.

The Competition

The 1st round will be held at APU's "gaming lab" (TL-04-05 & TL04-06) on the 21st of May, 2019, 6pm to 9pm, with subsequent matches taking place at the same place and time on the next day. Players are allowed to bring their own gaming peripherals. The progression map is shown below:
