About APU Extreme Esports Club

The club started from a humble beginning…

On June 10, 2016, the secondary school principal of club founder, Mr Lau, made a shocking announcement to consider including Esports as a club activity. He was pondering about the idea of setting up an esports club in the school, considering it started to gain traction as one of the official competitive sports in the world.


The announcement made by Mr Lau.

However, due to the lack of support from public, there is no further news development for the esports club getting established after the first post. Not giving up hope, the club founder created a secret group, named STEC (and subsequently renamed multiple times to avoid legal issues) which often held gaming sessions in school computer lab to sharpen the members esports skills.

As the club founder left his secondary school and started to study in Asia Pacific University, he realized that the University already started to include esports as part of the academic platform, while esports club is already a thing since forever. Therefore, with the intend of not causing conflict of interest, he founded the Extreme Esports club, which have similar objectives to achieve the same goal but very different ideology.
