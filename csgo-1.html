Recoil Control

It is important to control the recoil while spraying so that you can shoot accurately with the predictable spray pattern. Snipers are the only weapon category that doesn't have recoil as it can't use to spray.

Simple Recoil

Pistols and Shotguns recoil except XM1014 are mostly straightforward to compensate with only a vertical and slightly horizontal line of spray pattern. There are a few Pistols spray pattern & recoil compensation listed below:

Glock 18
Glock-18 Spray Pattern Glock-18 Recoil Compensation
P2000
P2000 Spray Pattern P2000 Recoil Compensation

Complex Recoil

Other than Pistols and Shotguns, weapons such as Assault Rifles (AR), Submachine Guns (SMG), Machine Guns, and Shotguns have massive differences in spray pattern. These weapons recoil control are more difficult to learn because its learning curve is steep as it takes in many factors to control the recoil. For instances, time and gaming peripherals such as Mouse and Mouse Pad. There are a few AR and SMG spray pattern & recoil compensation listed below:
